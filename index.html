Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our civil service exam preparation program.

How long does it take to prepare for the civil service exam?

Most students prepare for 2-3 months, but it depends on your starting knowledge and available study time. Our personalized study plans help you prepare efficiently based on your current level.

Will EksamCSC materials cover both Professional and Sub-professional levels?

Yes, we offer comprehensive materials for both Professional and Sub-professional civil service examinations, with specific modules tailored to each level.

How current are your study materials?

Our materials are updated regularly to reflect the latest exam patterns and question types. We analyze recent exams and adjust our content accordingly.

Can I access the materials on my mobile device?

Yes, all our study materials and practice tests are fully optimized for mobile devices, allowing you to study anytime, anywhere.

What if I don't pass the exam after using EksamCSC?

We offer a Pass Guarantee for our Professional and Premium plans. If you don't pass, you'll get extended access to our platform at no additional cost until you do.

How do the coaching sessions work?

Our coaching sessions are conducted live via video conferencing. Group sessions follow a set schedule, while one-on-one sessions can be scheduled at your convenience.
